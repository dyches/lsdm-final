Team ESCA

What it does:

Our team name is 'ESCA' which stands for food in Latin.

This website interacts with a database that contains the name of food products that are sold in the U.S., Mexico and UK.

How to use it:

  1. Enter food name in text field.
  2. Click Enter.
  3. Results will be given below.
